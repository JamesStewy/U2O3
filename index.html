Dr Rubina Hameed is a General Paediatrician. She works in the public system in the Allergy and Immunology Department at the Royal Children's Hospital. In private she consults at Main Road Specialist Centre, Eltham and Northpark Private Hospital, Bundoora.

She graduated from University of Punjab, Pakistan and worked in Pakistan, Saudi Arabia and United Kingdom. She trained in Paediatric Medicine in the United Kingdom where she obtained a Diploma of Child Health and MRCPI. She worked in the Birmingham Children's Hospital, UK, as a Paediatrician before moving to Melbourne, Australia in 2003. She is married to Paediatric Endocrinologist Dr Justin Brown and has three children.

Dr Hameed sees children from 0 to 18 years of age. She has special interest in infant mental health, infant feeding and allergies. She sees children for development, behaviour and Autism assessments.

Some of the other conditions she commonly sees include Eczema, Asthma, Constipation, Urinary Tract Infections and Adolescence related issues.

Qualifications: MBBS, DCH (London), MRCPI (Ireland), MRCPCH (UK), FRACP (Australia)
